About

Dr Sherry Bhalla is a computational biologist specializing in cancer genomics, bioinformatics, and machine learning-driven biomarker discovery. Her research integrates genomic, epigenomic, and proteomic data to identify molecular signatures that improve cancer diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment strategies.

Research Contributions

  • Analysis of large-scale TCGA genomic and epigenomic datasets to identify cancer stage biomarkers.
  • Development of machine learning tools including CancerTSP, CancerCSP, and CancerLSP.
  • Classification of primary vs metastatic skin tumors using gene expression, miRNA, and methylation data.
  • Development of CancerPDF database compiling experimentally identified cancer-associated peptides.
  • Multi-omics patient similarity network (MM-PSN) for multiple myeloma.
  • Proteogenomics research identifying aberrant peptides in metastatic breast cancer cell lines.
