Professional Summary

Dr. Isha Monga is a bioinformatics scientist with more than 13 years of experience applying computational genomics, machine learning, and single-cell analytics to biomedical research and therapeutic discovery. Her expertise includes single-cell transcriptomics, multi-omics integration, ligand-receptor communication, and systems-level disease modeling. She has contributed to large-scale projects in oncology, neuroscience, immunology, and dermatology at institutions such as **Weill Cornell Medicine, Mount Sinai, and Columbia University**. Her work bridges computational biology with translational research, enabling data-driven insights for target discovery and therapeutic development.
