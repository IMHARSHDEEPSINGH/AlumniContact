Professional Summary

Dr. Aprajita Gupta is a regulatory affairs professional and medical writer with expertise in EU MDR-based regulatory documentation for medical devices. Her work focuses on developing Clinical Evaluation Plans (CEPs), Clinical Evaluation Reports (CERs), literature review reports, and other regulatory documents that support clinical evaluations and compliance with international regulatory frameworks. She has extensive experience conducting systematic literature searches, analyzing scientific data, and preparing technical documentation for regulatory submissions. Her work involves close collaboration with interdisciplinary teams across academia, healthcare, and industry to ensure high-quality evidence-based regulatory documentation. In addition to regulatory writing, she has a strong research background in systems biology and bioinformatics, supported by practical laboratory experience in molecular biology and biomedical research techniques.
