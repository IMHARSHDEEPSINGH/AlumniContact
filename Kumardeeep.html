About

Dr Kumardeep Chaudhary is a computational biologist and data scientist specializing in clinical informatics, cancer bioinformatics, big data analytics, and artificial intelligence for healthcare. His work focuses on integrating multi-omics datasets and developing predictive computational models for precision medicine and complex disease biology.
